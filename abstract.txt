OCT-CANCER v3.0 — Lite Edition (Abstract)

This project presents a formal, conflict-based approach to cancer treatment using logical expressibility, semantic alignment, and structure-to-predicate resolution.

The Lite Edition includes:
- Core definition of the μ(S, Q) metric
- Δμ-based therapeutic planning principle
- A legal and semantic protection layer (license + ORCID)

This repository intentionally omits:
- Full logic engine implementations
- Patient-specific AI planner
- EF-game formulations and model-theoretic extensions
