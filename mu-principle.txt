OCT-CANCER v3.0 — μ-Principle

Let:
- S ∈ StructSpace: patient's biological system
- Q ∈ PredicateSpace: normative logic constraint
- φ(Q): logical encoding of Q in FOL, MSO, or FPC
- μ(S, Q): semantic distance between S and Q

Definition:
    μ(S, Q) = inf_{φ ∈ Logic(Q)} d(S, φ(Q))

Where:
- d is a logic-consistent distance over violations
